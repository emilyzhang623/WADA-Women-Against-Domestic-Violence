Domestic Violence Screening Quiz

1. Do you feel anxious or nervous when you are around your partner?




2. Do you watch what you are doing in order to avoid making your partner angry or upset?




3. Are you afraid of voicing a different opinion than your partner?




4. Does your partner criticize you or embarrass you in front of others?




5. Does your partner check up on what you have been doing, and not believe your answers?




6. Is your partner jealous, such as accusing you of having affairs?




7. Does your partner tell you that he or she will stop beating you when you start behaving yourself?




8. Have you stopped seeing your friends or family because of your partner’s behavior?



9. Does your partner’s behavior make you feel as if you are wrong?




10. Does your partner threaten to harm you?




11. Do you try to please your partner rather than yourself in order to avoid being hurt?




12. Does your partner keep you from going out or doing things that you want to do?




13. Do you feel that nothing you do is ever good enough for your partner?




14. Does your partner say that if you try to leave him or her, you will never see your children again?




15. Does your partner say that if you try to leave, he or she will kill himself or herself or you?




16. Is there always an excuse for your partner’s behavior? (“The alcohol or drugs made me do it! My job is too stressful! If dinner was on time I wouldn’t have hit you! I was just joking!”)




17. Are you forced to do things that you do not want to?




18. Do you lie to your family, friends and doctor about your bruises, cuts and scratches?
